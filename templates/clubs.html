Clubs and Communities

Various student committees are active on the campus and look after specific aspects of student activities. Alumni, branding, food, placement, sports, photography, theatre, art and cultural festival are some of the areas handled by various students’ committees. A group of students also voluntarily get together to pursue an extra curricular or co-curricular activity with some broadly defined goals, and form a student club.
